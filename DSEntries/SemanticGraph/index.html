Building a semantic network

| Complex Systems | Data Science | Music Evolution |

A semantic network, sometimes referred as knowledge graph is a graph G(v,e)\mathcal{G}(v,e)G(v,e) where the vertices (or nodes) represent concepts, entities, events, etc. and the edges represent a relationship between the concepts. This relationship is said to be semantic because in natural language process it is usually defined if the concepts appear in the same sentence, paragraph or document.
